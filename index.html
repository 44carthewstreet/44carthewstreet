Village Living Rural Setting

Tucked away in Ōkato, this is a home that strikes that sweet spot between relaxed rural living and everyday convenience. Set on a fully fenced section, there’s plenty of room to spread out — plus a large double garage with workshop space, an extra tool shed, and a separate sleepout that’s perfect for guests, teens, or a bit of creative overflow.

Inside, the house has been thoughtfully renovated and is neat and welcoming throughout. There are four generous bedrooms, a modern, functional kitchen, and a spacious living area centred around a woodfire that keeps things warm and cosy through the cooler months. The layout just works — practical, comfortable, and well-suited to family life, with contemporary finishes that feel fresh without trying too hard.

You can stroll into the heart of Ōkato village for schools, cafés and local shops, and New Plymouth is an easy 25-minute drive away. It’s a home that’s been genuinely cared for and steadily improved — ready for the next owners to move in and enjoy.
